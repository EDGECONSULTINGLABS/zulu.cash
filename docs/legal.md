## Non-Custodial Design

ZULU does not:
- Hold, custody, or control user funds
- Process payments on behalf of users
- Act as a money transmitter
- Require KYC/AML compliance

ZULU is a **view-only** tool for detecting shielded Zcash transactions using user-provided viewing keys.

## Privacy Compliance

ZULU is designed to comply with privacy regulations:

- **GDPR**: No personal data collection, processing, or storage on external servers
- **CCPA**: No sale of user data (we don't collect it in the first place)
- **Local-first**: All data remains on user's device under their control
